Chicken Oiled Rice

That is my special meal, the one i like most. You will find the flavor and smell most refreshing.
You'll find the full recipe below.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken breast
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • 3 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 3 Cucumbers
  • 3 table spoons of Soy sauce
  • 3 cups of vinegar
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 3 cups of rice

Steps

  1. First, rinse the chicken and place the chicken and 3 cloves of garlic into a pot and boil them for 30 mins.
  2. Fry the 1 clove of garlic. Put the boiled stock and broth to the pot with rice. Cook the rice with medium heat till water is gone.
  3. Put the rice in a plate and enjoy it!
